Meet Beautiful Bear

Bear is a male tabby aged somewhere between 2 & 3 years old, who just loves to be petted and fussed.

He is a gentle boy who will reward you with purrs and head butts.

We rescued Bear from the streets, where a kind lady had been feeding him. He is very loving and trusting boy with a really good appetite.

At the moment, due  to his poor start in life, where he was fighting for survival,  he has no idea of how to play. I'm certain that once settled in a loving forever home, that will come in time.

We are looking for a home, where Bear will be kept as an indoor only cat.  We believe that he would make a perfect companion cat.

He has been, snap tested for Fiv & FeLV,(all negative), neutered, microchipped had his first vaccination including FeLV. (Feline leukaemia) plus has had both flea and worm treatments.

If you would like to meet Bear with a view to offering him a home, please complete the CAT ADOPTION FORM.
