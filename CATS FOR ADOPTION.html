Midnight & Dora

These two beautiful girls came to us because their owner was poorly.  We are hoping to keep them together if possible and they will need access to an outdoor garden that is safe, away from main roads, they are 8 and 6 years old.  If the right home came for an indoor home, we believe this would also be a possibility for them.

They love been picked up and cuddled and they will reward you with happy purrs. They need stability and to be part of a loving family. They have brilliant appetites and fully litter trained, absolutely no trouble at all.


They enjoy a brush and their fur is absolutely beautiful. They are fully vaccinated including against leukaemia and ready to go to their loving forever home.

If you would like to offer these two loving beauties a home, please click on the link at the end of this post and complete the Adoption Form.
