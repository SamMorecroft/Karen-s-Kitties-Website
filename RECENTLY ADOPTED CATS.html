Tiggy

Tiggy is a beautiful British Shorthair of around 18 years old. Her owner died leaving many animals.

The family managed to home all the other pets, but Tiggy was so shut down and traumatised that she was left at a local vets.

They contacted us and asked for help. We fostered her for a few weeks and she showed no interest in anything.

One of our memberes contacted us and offered to take her on as a foster, with a view to adopt her if things worked out.

Fortunately this arrangement worked out perfectly. The results have been amazing, Tiggy loves her new Mum and her foster brother, a beautiful litter Persian cat called Harry. She has learnt to relax, love and trust again. She is certainly living her best life.

Tiggy
